A simulation framework for exploring job scheduling and memory-allocation strategies in HPC systems with **disaggregated memory resources**, developed during my internships at Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory.

The simulator underpins results published at **CLUSTER'24** and **IPDPSW'24**, and is used in ongoing collaborations on memory-disaggregated system design.
