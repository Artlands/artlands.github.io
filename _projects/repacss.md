**REPACSS** (Renewable Energy Powered Advanced Computing Systems and Services) is an NSF Category II award ($12.25M) building a large-scale computing facility powered by renewable energy at Texas Tech University.

I co-designed and built the cluster from the ground up — contributing to hardware selection, data center monitoring, remote management, and system software — and continue to work on its operation and performance optimization.

Learn more at [repacss.org](https://repacss.org/).
